Procedure to become a LIC Agent

Documents

  • 3 Colour passport size photo.
  • Photo copy of 12th marksheet/ Diploma marksheet/ Degree certificate.
  • Photo copy of Pan card.

Fees

  • Rs. 150 for registration.
  • Rs.250 for online training fees {no fees for offline training}.
  • Rs.350 for online exam.
  • Rs.125 for license of IRDA.
  • Total you have pay Rs. 875.

Registration & Training

Upon successful registration, you will be undergoing 50-Hrs Online Training to make you aware of theoretical aspects of Life Insurance which is very much required from the examination point of view. In addition to ONLINE TRAINING, we would be providing you practical training at your convenient timings to make you aware of Marketing skills needed to succeed in your Career with LIC.

Exam

After successful completion of training you will have to sit for pre-licensing online examination conducted by the insurance regulatory and development authority (IRDA). Exam will be of 50 marks and question will be objective. You have to get 17 marks to pass exam.

License

After successful completion of the examination you will be awarded a license by the IRDA to work as an insurance agent. You will be appointed as an agent by our branch office and you will be a part of the team under development officer.
